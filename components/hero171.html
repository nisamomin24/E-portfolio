Launching into Cybersecurity

The Introduction to Cybersecurity module is designed to provide me with a foundational understanding of the key principles and practices in the field of cybersecurity. This course covers essential topics such as the history and evolution of cybersecurity, common threats and vulnerabilities, and the fundamental techniques used to protect information systems. I will learn about various types of cyber attacks, including malware, phishing, and denial-of-service attacks, and how to identify and mitigate these threats. The module also introduces the concept of ethical hacking and penetration testing, emphasizing the importance of proactive security measures and continuous monitoring. The module also emphasizes the importance of cybersecurity policies and governance. I will learn about legal and regulatory frameworks, risk management, and the development of security policies and incident response plans. Case studies and practical exercises will help me apply theoretical knowledge to real-world scenarios, preparing me to address cybersecurity challenges effectively.
